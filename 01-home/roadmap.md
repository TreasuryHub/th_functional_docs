# Roadmap & Your Treasury Journey

> **Availability:** `Available` ✅\* (the roadmap screen is present in the build; the interactive **Alex** Customer Success agent is `In Preview` 👁️)
> **Where to find it:** Roadmap
> **Who uses it:** treasury leadership, project owners, and administrators planning their rollout.
> **Permissions required:** standard platform access — no special permission to view (see [Roles & Permissions](../00-getting-started/04-roles-and-permissions.md)).

\* = present in the current build; scope should be confirmed by the Treasury Hub team (see your administrator).

## Overview
The Roadmap screen shows you **what Treasury Hub has already shipped, what's coming next, and how
that maps onto your own rollout plan**. It answers two questions in one place: "What can the platform
do?" and "What do *we* still need to switch on to reach our goals?"

It's organized into two tabs — a **Product Roadmap** (a change log plus a forward-looking roadmap)
and **Your Treasury Journey** (your milestones, each mapped to the features that deliver them).

## Key concepts
- **Change Log ("What's New")** — a dated, versioned list of recently released capabilities, updated
  as new versions ship.
- **Roadmap** — planned work laid out across upcoming quarters, grouped by area (Integrations,
  Reconciliation, Reporting, Workflows, Agents), with each item marked **Shipped**, **In
  Development**, or **Planned**.
- **Milestone** — a goal in your own rollout (e.g. "Payment Hub activation", "PSP integration"),
  placed on a timeline phase.
- **Feature mapping** — every milestone is broken into the specific features that deliver it, each
  flagged **Available** ✅ or **In Preview** 👁️, so you always know what's ready today.
- **Alex (AI)** — the Customer Success agent who, in preview, will manage the bridge between your
  requirements and the product roadmap. *(In Preview — see below.)*

## How to use it

### See what's shipped and what's coming
1. Open **Roadmap** from the sidebar and select the **Product Roadmap** tab.
2. Read the **Change Log** ("What's New") for recently released versions and improvements.
3. Review the **Roadmap** lanes to the right — each area (Integrations, Reconciliation, Reporting,
   Workflows, Agents) shows planned items across the coming quarters.
4. Use the legend to read status at a glance: **Shipped**, **In Development**, **Planned**.

### Track your rollout on Your Treasury Journey
1. Select the **Your Treasury Journey** tab.
2. Follow the timeline across the phases — typically **Onboarding → Foundation → Expansion →
   Optimization → Transformation**.
3. Open a **milestone card** to see its feature mapping: which features are **Available** ✅ today and
   which are **In Preview** 👁️.
4. Check the **progress indicator** to see how many of your mapped features are already live.

> The versions, quarters, milestones, and progress scores shown in demos are **illustrative
> examples**. Your own journey and the live change log reflect your organization's actual rollout.

## Configuration
- The **Change Log and Roadmap** are maintained by Treasury Hub and update as releases ship — there
  is nothing for you to configure.
- **Milestones** in Your Treasury Journey are tailored to your organization. Today they are set up
  with your account team; in the roadmap version you'll be able to add and edit them yourself through
  Alex.

## Tips & good practices
- Use **Your Treasury Journey** in planning meetings to agree what to activate next and to see which
  goals are already achievable with **Available** ✅ features.
- Check the **Active** / **Upgrade** tags in the platform (or with your administrator) to confirm what's
  live for your organization versus what's a premium plan or in preview.
- Check the **Change Log** periodically so your team adopts new capabilities as they ship.
